Coal Barbecues is a Casual Dining restaurant with tabletop cooking serving a variety of cuisines. Although they are famous for their Arabian, they also serve North Indian, Mediterranean, Asian and Barbecue dishes. One gets to enjoy mouth-watering barbecue and grilled dishes. Apart from the a-la-carte menu, they have a wide array of choices in the buffet spread as well. Coal Barbecues has a peppy and vibrant ambiance. Coal Barbecues is located in Thyagaraya Nagar, Central Chennai
